1. What's Mental Health Org?

  • Mental Health org is a platform That depends on volunteers. It was developed by Hana Salama as her graduation project from Code Like a Girl program. It will provide resources and support to individuals who are struggling with mental health issues. It will also serve as a place for people to connect with mental health professionals and peer support groups for free.

    • 2. Features

  • Mental Health Assessments: Users will be able to take online assessments to determine their level of mental health and get personalized recommendations.
  • Mental Health Professionals Directory: Our website will have a directory of mental health professionals, including therapists, psychologists, and psychiatrists. Users will be able to search for professionals based on their location, specialty, and availability.
  • Peer Support Groups: Users will be able to join peer support groups where they can connect with others who are going through similar experiences.
  • Mental Health Resources: We will provide a variety of mental health resources, including articles, videos, and podcasts, to help users better understand mental health issues and learn coping strategies.

    • 3. Privacy and Security

  • We take the privacy and security of our users very seriously. All user data will be encrypted and stored securely. We will also use two-factor authentication to ensure that only authorized users can access sensitive information.

    • 4. Conclusion

  • Mental Health Org will provide a safe and supportive environment for individuals who are struggling with mental health issues. We believe that by providing access to mental health resources and support, we can make a positive impact on the lives of many people.
